A Peek Into the Life of E.T.?

Tyler Guerrero, Emanuel Padilla, Cameron Snyder

Greetings, Traveler

  • The data used is a CSV from Kaggle based on Extraterrestial Reports from 1910-2009. It shows location, time, shapes of the extraterrestial, and brief descriptions of the event.

  • Link to UFO Sightings Around the World

Is it a bird? Is it a plane? Superman?

  • In this analysis, we’ll hunt for potential UFO “hotspots” on Earth—places where sightings cluster—so you could, in theory, grab some friends, camp out, and go UFO‑watching in high‑probability zones.

The questions that we seek answers for:

  • Where should you vacation to if you want a chance at meeting E.T?
  • Is there a country that best suits this need?
  • What time of year does E.T. prefer to travel?
  • Is there a particular time of day that E.T. leaves his house?
  • Could E.T. be among us at this very moment?

TOOLS USED:

  • Data Manipulation:

  • deplyr, tidyverse, and lubridate were used to clean up our data by converting strings to numeric values, as well as allowing us to manipulate the data to find answers.

  • Data Visualization & Aesthetics:

  • ggplot2, ggridges, RColorBrewer, viridis, ggrepel, ggthemes, grid, gridExtra all helped us to make our presentation a little nicer looking.
